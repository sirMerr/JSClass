Memory Game

Click on or press on the corresponding letter of two tileso turn them face up. If they are of the same then you win the pair and play again until all tilesre turned. If they are not the same, they are turned face down again.
Click on the Reset button to reset the game or the End button if you are unable to solve a 4x4 matching game.
The cheat button will allow you to see the second layer of the board.
